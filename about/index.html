This site

This site is primarily a personally maintained repository of my thoughts and writing. I am a fledgling essay author. Forgive my poor writing skills, irregularity, verbosity, etc that may make you leave. I hope you come back to read more when I get better.

The previous iteration was made while learning Ruby on Rails. The one before that was made while learning basic web technologies, HTML, CSS, js, etc. Now that I've learned those things, I have decided a static page is best for personal sites. Jekyll is my weapon of choice, and I happily vouch for it.

I started working on this in Thailand, on April 22nd 2014.
