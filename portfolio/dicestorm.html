Summary

Website; Core team members: 2; February 2011 - Current

Operation Dicestorm is a unique board-game that we are aiming to crowdfund for our first release in 2015.

The design focuses on providing different levels of gameplay depth for different players. We do this by emphasizing three factors to performance - strategy, dexterity and chance - which are intricately interwoven.

The game is heavily reliant on its physical aspect, making it nearly impossible to recreate digitally. This encourages people to play it face-to-face, the true form in which board games were meant to be enjoyed!
