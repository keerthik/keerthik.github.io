At a Glance

Summary

Website; Team size: 2; February 2011 - Current

Operation Dicestorm is a unique board-game geared towards being an irreplaceable physical and social experience with different levels of gameplay depth for different players.

The game is heavily reliant on its physical aspect, making it nearly impossible to recreate digitally. This encourages people to play it face-to-face, the true form in which board games were meant to be enjoyed!
